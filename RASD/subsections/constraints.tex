\begin{itemize}
		\item [C1] Logging in whitout being signed up is prevented. (f1)
		\item [C2] Logging in, being already logged in, is impossible. (f1)
		\item [C3] Signing up, being already signed up, is impossible.(f1)
		\item [C4] Creating a meeting with the same schedule of an existent one is not allowed. (f2)
		\item [C5] Scheduling a meeting during break pauses, setted in preferences, is prevented. (f2)
		\item [C6] it is impossible to generate an appointment in the past or in an invalid date. (f2)
		\item [C7] Meeting creation requires name,date,hour,location and a starting point to be defined properly.(f2)
		\item [C8] At least one mean of travel must be selected.(f3)
		\item [C9] Every lunch break must last at least 30 minutes.(f3)
		\item [C10] If rain or snow are in the forecast, travel by bus is preferred. (f6)
		\item [C11] In case of strikes, routes involving the related travel mean are not considered. (f6)
		\item [C12] Reminders must be setted before the scheduled time of the related events(f7)
	\end{itemize}
